What It Does

AI-powered contract review, built for professionals

Easy As PAI analyses contracts in minutes, surfacing risks, flagging non-standard clauses, and producing structured summaries — so your team can focus on decisions, not documents.

Clause Identification

Instantly identifies and categorises every clause — liability, indemnity, termination, confidentiality, payment terms — across any contract type.

Risk Flagging

Highlights clauses that deviate from standard market positions or your preferred playbook, ranked by severity so nothing critical is missed.

Structured Summaries

Produces a clear, structured summary of every contract — key dates, obligations, rights, and risks — ready to share with stakeholders in seconds.

Playbook Comparison

Upload your standard playbook and Easy As PAI will measure every incoming contract against it, showing exactly where it falls short.

Multi-Party Contracts

Handles complex multi-party agreements, joint ventures, and framework contracts — tracking obligations and rights across all signatories.

Secure & Confidential

Your documents never train the model. End-to-end encryption and enterprise-grade security keeps sensitive contracts completely confidential.

How It Works

From upload to insight in minutes

01

Upload your contract

Simply upload your contract in PDF or Word format. Easy As PAI accepts any contract type — NDAs, service agreements, employment contracts, commercial leases, supply chain agreements and more.

02

AI review runs instantly

Our professional AI engine reads the full document, identifies every clause, compares it against market standards and your playbook, and flags any risks — all in a matter of minutes.

03

Receive your report

Get a clear, structured report with a full clause breakdown, risk summary, and recommended actions. Share it directly with your team or export to Word or PDF.

04

Lawyer reviews & advises

Where needed, an RPC lawyer reviews the AI output, adds legal judgement, and provides advice — combining the speed of AI with the expertise of one of the UK's leading law firms.
